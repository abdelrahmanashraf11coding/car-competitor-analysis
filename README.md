# AI-Powered Competitor Analysis - Car Sales

Analysis of 23,906 car sales transactions to identify market trends and competitors.

## What's Analyzed
- Top 10 companies by sales volume
- Average price per brand
- Sales distribution by region
- Most popular body styles

## Key Insights
- Chevrolet, Dodge & Ford dominate sales
- Austin is the strongest region (17.3%)
- SUV & Hatchback are most in-demand
- Cadillac has the highest average price (~$40K)

## Tech Stack
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib & Seaborn
- OpenPyXL
- Dataset from Kaggle (23,906 records)
